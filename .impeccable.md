## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are founders, strategists, product teams, and researchers exploring macro trends shaping 2026. They use this tool during ideation, planning, and decision framing when they need fast access to connected evidence rather than isolated reports.

### Brand Personality
Signal-rich, exploratory, and credible. The interface should feel like a modern foresight studio: clear enough for decision-making, but distinctive enough to invite deep exploration.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial intelligence-lab style with warm, paper-like tones, high-contrast typography, and data-map atmospherics. Avoid generic dark-neon AI aesthetics. Favor expressive but disciplined visuals, with motion used for orientation and focus.

### Design Principles
1. Show relationships before summaries; make connectivity the first-class interaction.
2. Keep every query grounded in visible evidence and page-level citations.
3. Balance strategic depth with fast scanning through hierarchy, spacing, and concise copy.
4. Build for exploration on both desktop and mobile without removing core capabilities.
5. Preserve trust through readable contrast, clear states, and predictable interactions.
