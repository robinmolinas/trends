## Summary

A small business-oriented trends piece from Lovable (a no-code app builder), aggregating data from US Chamber of Commerce, Salesforce, SBA, Gartner, and others. While primarily a marketing piece for their platform, it synthesizes usable data on SMB AI adoption, the no-code/low-code shift, cybersecurity vulnerability, and social commerce. The data is third-party sourced and largely consistent with enterprise-level research.

The key commercial insight: capabilities that once required enterprise budgets are now accessible to small businesses — AI tools, no-code development (70-95% cost reduction), personalization at scale (under $50/month). The competitive gap between large and small businesses is narrowing in tooling while widening in data and brand.

## Key Claims

- 58% of small businesses now use generative AI (up from 40% in 2024, 23% in 2023) — US Chamber of Commerce
- 75% of SMBs actively investing in AI tools; over one-third have fully integrated AI into daily operations — Salesforce
- 82% of small businesses using AI increased their workforce over the past year
- No-code/low-code development: 70% cost reduction, 90% faster project completion vs. traditional development
- 71% of consumers expect personalized interactions; 76% frustrated when they don't get them — McKinsey
- 81% of small businesses suffered a security breach or data breach in the past year — Identity Theft Resource Center
- 55% of SMBs say a $50,000 cyber loss would cause the business to fail
- TikTok Shop sales increased 120% in the 12 months to June 2025 (TikTok Newsroom)
- Zendesk: customer-centric brands report profits 60% higher than those not focused on CX; 22% higher customer retention, 33% higher acquisition rates
- Revenue-based financing grew 70.9% YoY, reaching $5.78B market

## Data Points

- 58% of SMBs using gen AI (US Chamber of Commerce, 2026)
- 70% development cost reduction from no-code platforms
- 81% of SMBs suffered a security breach in the past year
- $50,000: Threshold at which 55% of SMBs would fail from a cyberattack
- TikTok Shop GMV: +120% YoY to June 2025
- Revenue-based financing: $5.78B market, growing to $41.8B by 2028
