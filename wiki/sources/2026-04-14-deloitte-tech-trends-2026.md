## Summary

Deloitte's 17th annual Tech Trends report (72 pages) frames 2026 as the inflection point where AI moves from proof-of-concept to production at scale. Last year's prediction — that AI would become "akin to electricity" — is now proven. Five trends are identified: Physical AI (robots becoming adaptive), Agentic Reality Check (most implementations automate rather than redesign), AI Infrastructure Reckoning (cloud costs becoming prohibitive for high-volume), The Great Rebuild (redesigning tech organizations as AI-native), and AI Dilemma (cybersecurity paradox).

The "agentic reality check" is the most important counterweight to the hype. Despite early enthusiasm, only 11% of surveyed organizations have deployed agentic systems in production. Most failures stem from automating existing broken processes rather than fundamentally redesigning operations for AI-native execution. The insight: agentic AI requires process redesign, not process automation.

## Key Claims

- Only 11% of organizations have deployed agentic systems in production; most still automate rather than redesign
- Physical AI is evolving robots from preprogrammed → adaptive, autonomous systems; industrial robots, autonomous vehicles, drones entering mainstream
- Humanoid robots projected: 2 million workplace humanoids by 2035
- AI token costs have dropped substantially, but *total AI spending is exploding* due to massive usage growth — organizations hitting $10M+/month cloud bills
- Strategic hybrid architecture emerging: cloud for variable workloads, on-premises for consistent production inference, edge for latency-critical
- 64% of organizations increasing AI investments and tech budgets
- New roles emerging: AI collaboration designers, edge AI engineers, prompt engineers, CIOs evolving to AI orchestrators
- AI creates a cybersecurity paradox: same capabilities driving innovation also introducing new risks (shadow AI, model poisoning, data exfiltration)
- GenAI reached ~100M users in 2 months vs 50 years for telephones to reach 50M — exponential adoption flywheel

## Data Points

- 11% of organizations with agentic AI in production
- 64% increasing AI investments
- Monthly AI cloud bills reaching tens of millions for large organizations
- 100M users in 2 months (GenAI adoption speed vs historical technology benchmarks)
