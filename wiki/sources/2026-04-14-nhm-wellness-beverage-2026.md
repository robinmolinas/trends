## Summary

A trade-oriented wellness beverage trends guide from Advantage Solutions (a major US consumer goods sales agency), providing retailer and brand-facing intelligence on the functional beverage category. While narrower than other sources, it offers precise market data on one of the fastest-growing FMCG segments and reveals Gen Z and Millennial consumer behavior around functional health. Connects to the broader wellness optimization trend through the consumer-facing lens of what people are actually putting in their shopping carts.

## Key Claims

- The functional beverage category stands at $92B with projections to reach $106.93B by 2026 — beverages are no longer just refreshment but "targeted wellness solutions that function like supplements"
- Gut health beverages posted 34.1% sales growth in 2024 and are the fastest-growing health positioning in functional beverages (NBJ)
- Functional soft drink purchases surged 108.8% across US retail channels in 2024 — outpacing all other functional beverage categories by significant margins (NIQ)
- Brain health/cognitive wellness: $89.4M segment, growing from 6% in 2024 to over 9% by 2028 (NBJ)
- Functional sports protein beverages projected at 9.5% sales growth by 2028
- 65% of Gen Z and 62% of Millennials are actively seeking calming and relaxing claims in beverages
- The clean-label revolution: consumers demand transparency and reduced sugar content; premium protein beverages in "steady expansion"
- AI-assisted shopping lists are making it "easier than ever to optimize nutrient intake through food and beverage choices" — AI is entering the functional nutrition purchase decision
