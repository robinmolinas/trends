## Summary
Released September 16, 2025, Huawei's Intelligent World 2035 Report (product of 200+ workshops, 100+ expert interviews over two years) identifies 10 tech megatrends shaping the next decade. Paired with a Global Digitalization and Intelligence Index (GDII) that maps countries' digital economies using a "digital production factor" framework (data, ICT talent, digital/intelligent technologies). The report's worldview is maximally bullish on AGI timelines and AI-physical integration, reflecting Huawei's infrastructure and network positioning.

## Key Claims
- **Megatrend 1**: AGI will be the largest driver of economic transformation; integrating AI with the physical world is essential to achieving AGI, which will lead to a technological singularity
- **Megatrend 2**: AI agents will transform from simple execution tools into decision-making partners
- **Megatrend 3**: Human-AI programming will reshape software development; AI handles repetitive coding while humans focus on top-level design and creative thinking
- **Megatrend 4**: Human-computer interaction moves from graphical UIs to natural language + multimodal interfaces (voice, gesture, full sensory experience)
- **Megatrend 5**: Mobile apps transition from isolated function units to service nodes driven by AI agents
- **Megatrend 6**: World models will enable Level 4+ autonomous vehicles that serve as "mobile third space"
- **Megatrend 7**: Global computing capacity will increase 100,000-fold by 2035 via post-von Neumann architectures (new semiconductor materials, process techniques, computing architectures, paradigms)
- **Megatrend 8**: AI storage demand will increase 500× vs. 2025 levels; AI storage will account for >70% of global total storage
- **Megatrend 9**: Nine billion humans will be connected to 900 billion agents, evolving mobile internet to "agentic internet"
- **Megatrend 10**: Global data centers will consume ~1.5 trillion kWh of electricity by 2035; this energy constraint will accelerate AI-managed energy grids; wind and solar will reach 50% of global energy mix by 2035

**AI social predictions by 2035:**
- AI will help prevent >80% of chronic diseases through proactive monitoring
- >90% of Chinese homes will have intelligent robots
- AI will be applied by >85% of companies and improve productivity 60%
