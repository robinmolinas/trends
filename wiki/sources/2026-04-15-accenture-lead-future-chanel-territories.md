## Summary
An Accenture strategic consulting deliverable prepared for Chanel (dated 03.04.2025), presenting three "territories of opportunity" as output from a discovery phase. The document is largely redacted (placeholder "Xxxxx" text throughout the commercial and consumer assessments) but the framework and territory rationale are visible. The three territories represent different bets on how Chanel could "lead the future of beauty" given shifting consumer behavior, particularly the rise of GenAI as a discovery and purchase channel.

Territory #1 (Post-Website Era) is the most digitally forward: Chanel should develop GenAI-native experiences to handle immersion, discovery, purchase, and engagement, consolidating data and preparing its IT architecture for an AI-mediated commerce era. This is an explicit acknowledgment that the website era is ending and brand presence must migrate to where AI agents operate. Territory #2 (Revamp Loyalty & Relationship) acknowledges that conversion-optimization killed brand loyalty (consistent with frog's New Loyalty research showing 2024 as the worst CX decade for US consumers). Territory #3 (Beauty-as-a-Service) proposes a services revenue model alongside traditional product sales.

Note: This is a confidential client deliverable with most supporting evidence redacted. The strategic directions are directionally valuable as signals of where consulting intelligence is pointing luxury beauty incumbents.

## Key Claims
- Chanel has three primary territories to "lead the future of beauty": Post-Website Era, Revamp Loyalty, and Beauty-as-a-Service
- Territory #1: GenAI-native client experiences for immersion, discovery, purchase, engagement — framing the website as obsolete infrastructure
- Territory #2: Loyalty transformation from conversion-optimization to "known and understood" — acknowledging CX collapse
- Territory #3: Beauty-as-a-Service — recurring service revenue alongside product sales
- The "post-website era" framing directly aligns with the Searchless Discovery and agentic commerce trends
