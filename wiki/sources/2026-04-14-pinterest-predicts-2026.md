## Summary

Pinterest's annual "not-yet-trending" report identifies 21 emergent micro-trends for 2026, derived from Pinterest internal search data (global, English language, September 2023 – August 2025). Pinterest's methodology is distinctive: rather than reporting what's currently trending, it surfaces search patterns that are growing from a small base — predicting what will go mainstream in the year ahead. The report spans food and drink, fashion, beauty, home décor, travel, celebrations, hobbies, and well-being.

The 21 trends identified for 2026 include: Afrohemian Decor (African + Bohemian home fusion), Brooched (vintage brooches as men's accessories), Cool Blue (subzero aesthetics in fashion and food), Cabbage Crush (cabbage replacing cauliflower in cooking), Darecations (daring adventure travel), and Mystic Outlands (mystical outdoor aesthetics). The broader cultural signals across the 21 trends include: Neo-Deco design revival; opera aesthetic crossover into fashion; glitchy digital-meets-analog aesthetics; and scent stacking (multi-perfume layering).

A recurring theme across Pinterest Predicts is the divergence from global monoculture — the trends skew toward hyperlocal cultural aesthetics, unconventional combinations, and Gen Z/Boomer behavior overlaps that defy demographic stereotypes.

## Key Claims

- 21 trend predictions for 2026, all based on growing search patterns from Pinterest's global platform
- Afrohemian Decor: afrobohemian home decor searches +220%, adire fabric +130%, motif berbere +210%
- Brooched (men's brooch fashion): brooch for men suit +90%, maximalist accessories +105%
- Cool Blue aesthetic: frosted make-up +150%, icy blue +50%, blue drinks aesthetic +55%
- Cabbage Crush (food): cabbage dumplings +110%, golumpki soup +95%, fermented cabbage +35%
- Darecations (adventure travel): growing search category departing from beach vacation norms
- Multiple trends show Boomer and Millennial adoption of trends previously associated with Gen Z
- Scent Stacking (layering multiple perfumes): emerging beauty trend
- Opera Aesthetic: crossing from cultural event into fashion/lifestyle trend
- FunHaus: creative, playful home design aesthetic

## Data Points

- Source: Pinterest internal search data, global English language, September 2023 – August 2025
- Method: normalized search growth comparing September 2024–August 2025 vs. prior year period
- Demographic data: Gen Z (18–27), Millennials (28–43), Gen X (44–59), Baby Boomer (60+)

## Contradictions / Tensions

- Pinterest Predicts is fundamentally a marketing tool for the Pinterest platform, designed to convince brands to advertise against these emerging audiences. The trends are real (based on search data), but the framing as "predictions" obscures that these are already-happening search behaviors being amplified as predictions.
