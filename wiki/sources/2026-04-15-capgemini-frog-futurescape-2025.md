## Summary
Published by frog (part of Capgemini Invent), Futurescape 2025 maps 25 trends across a 25-year horizon organized around five questions: what behaviors will be replaced (Ebb & Flow), how will tech, culture and business impact people and planet (Forces of Nature), how will AI affect us in ways we're not prepared for (Machine Visions), which traditionally disparate worlds will intersect (Merging Dimensions), and what challenges will we need to overcome to stay ahead (New Horizons).

Unlike annual trend reports, this is a longer-arc foresight document identifying structural shifts in consumption, work, health, identity, and societal organization through roughly 2050.

## Key Claims

**Ebb & Flow (what's being replaced):**
- **Club Clarity**: Younger generations drinking less → rise of alcohol-free third spaces, supper clubs, biohacking communities. Running clubs and wellness meet-ups replacing happy hour.
- **Effortless is the New Normal**: Agent-led conversational commerce replaces scroll-and-search. AI personal agents anticipate needs, handle weekly shopping, assist with major purchases. Agentforce (Salesforce) and Rufus (Amazon) mark the agentic enterprise beginning.
- **Say No to Technicolor Overload**: E-Ink devices, grayscale phone screens, AI content filters to reduce cognitive load. Intentional quiet products as market category.
- **Time to Take Action**: Brands pivoting from "purpose-washing" to measurable impact; customers no longer buy abstract purpose promises.

**Forces of Nature:**
- **Expanding the C-Suite**: Non-human AI entering leadership decision-making structures (Chief AI Officer, AI board observers).
- **The Digital Planetary**: Technology reframed as human right and infrastructure path, not consumer good.
- **Biodesign in Full Bloom**: Biology and design converging — living materials, grown architecture, biodesigned products.

**Machine Visions:**
- **The Selective Trust Policy**: AI trust granted selectively and contextually — not blanket adoption.
- **Finding Ourselves in AI**: Identity formation and self-understanding through AI interaction.
- **Me and My Cyber Self**: Digital twins and AI-mediated self-reflection becoming personal tools.

**Merging Dimensions:**
- **Body Engaging in Our Natural Language**: Gesture, voice, gaze, and breath as primary interfaces ahead of keyboard/touch.
- **The Mind-Body Program**: Convergence of mental and physical health markets; holistic wellness as standard.

**New Horizons:**
- **Unleashing Zero UI**: Disappearance of visible interface layers as embedded ambient AI.
- **Building Emotional Resonance**: Emotional intelligence as core product/service differentiator.
- **The Power of the Parasocial**: Creator-audience relationships and brand-fan communities as commercial architecture.
