## Overview

Accenture Song is the experience and design arm of Accenture, operating across 40+ design studios and creative agencies globally. It publishes the annual **Life Trends** report — one of the most methodologically rigorous consumer trend reports available, combining qualitative interviews, large-scale global surveys (25,000+ respondents), executive surveys (800+), and netnographic analysis (700M+ online mentions). Key authors: Nick Law (Creative Chairperson), Katie Burke (Global Thought Leadership Lead), Jake Brody (Senior MD), Agneta Björnsjö (Global Research Lead).
